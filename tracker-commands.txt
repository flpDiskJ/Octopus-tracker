00 = arpeggio
01 = pitch up
02 = pitch down
03 = portamento
04 = vibrato
05 = echo (param 1 = number of re-triggers  param 2 = speed)
06 = multi-trigger
07 = tremolo
08 = hold and decay
09 = sample offset (scaled to sample size)
0A = reverse playback with offset
0B = playing sequence position jump
0C = set channel volume
0D = volume slide (param 1 = increase, param 2 = decrease)
0E = set channel pan. (param 1 = left level, param 2 = right level)

18 = set volume (relative to sample volume)

19 = sample offset (Octamed style)

0FFF = stop sound in channel
