XX = parameter

00XX = arpeggio
01XX = pitch up
02XX = pitch down
03XX = portamento
04XX = vibrato
(05 delay, 06 retrigger)
07XX = Tremolo
08XX = Hold and Decay
09XX = sample offset (scaled to sample size)
0AXX = reverse playback with sample offset
0BXX = playing sequence position jump
0CXX = set volume
0DXX = volume slide (param 1 = increase, param 2 = decrease)

18XX = set volume (relative to sample volume)

19XX = sample offset (Octamed style)

0FFF = kill sound in channel
